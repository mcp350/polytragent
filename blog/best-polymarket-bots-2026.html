Yes, though it requires coordination. Running multiple bots simultaneously on the same capital can lead to conflicting orders. We recommend using one primary bot and potentially a secondary for specific strategies.

MarketSignal has the longest proven track record (3+ years), but Polytragent has the best 2025-2026 returns. Newer doesn't mean worse—it means less historical data but potentially better current optimization.
