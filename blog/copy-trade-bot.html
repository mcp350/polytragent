Risk Considerations

Copy trading isn't risk-free. Be aware of:

  • Trader Risk: The traders you copy can lose money. No bot prevents bad trading decisions
  • Market Risk: Prediction markets are volatile. Prices can move dramatically on news
  • Correlation Risk: Multiple traders might have correlated strategies, increasing portfolio risk
  • Execution Risk: Network delays or exchange issues could cause slippage
  • Systemic Risk: Polymarket exchange downtime or regulatory action could freeze accounts
  • Trader Withdrawal Risk: If a trader you've been copying quits trading, you lose that revenue stream

Mitigate these by diversifying across 4-5 traders with uncorrelated strategies, maintaining realistic return expectations, monitoring trader performance monthly, and never betting more than 5% per trader.
