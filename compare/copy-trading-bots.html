Why Research Beats Copy Trading

Copy trading bots solve an emotional problem—they remove the paralysis of decision-making. But they create a profitability problem.

The Copy Trading Trap

When you copy successful wallets, you're executing yesterday's alpha. By the time you copy a trade, smart money has already positioned. Copy trading is fastest; but all traders are copying the same wallets. This means:

  • Crowded trades: Everyone copies at once, pushing odds further into inefficiency
  • Delayed entry: The best traders already profited; you're catching the tail
  • Momentum betting: You're buying momentum, not edge
  • No analysis: You don't know why you're trading—neither does the bot
