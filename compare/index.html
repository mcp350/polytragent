A tool with shallow market analysis will miss mispricings that AI research uncovers. Spending 30 minutes comparing tools now saves you from months of suboptimal returns.

AI Research vs. Copy Trading: The Core Tradeoff

The biggest divide in Polymarket tools is philosophical: do you want a bot that THINKS or a bot that COPIES?

Copy trading bots (PolyCop, PolyGun, OkBet) follow successful traders blindly. Pros: simple, proven track records from the traders you follow. Cons: pays recurring fees (PolyCop charges 0.5% per trade), works only when your chosen traders are active, misses independent mispricings, and doesn't scale to new market conditions.

AI research tools (Polytragent, Polysight, Alphascope) analyze market fundamentals, on-chain data, sentiment, and historical patterns. Pros: identify mispricings independently, no per-trade fees, adaptable to new markets, tell you WHY a market is mispriced. Cons: require more computational resources, depend on training data quality, and can be wrong if fundamentals shift unexpectedly.

For serious traders in 2026, the trend is clear: research-driven tools outperform copy bots because they identify edges that haven't yet been arbitraged away. PolyCop and similar tools work best as a COMPLEMENT to your own research, not as a primary strategy.
