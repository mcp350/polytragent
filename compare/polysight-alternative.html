Why Traders Are Looking for Polysight Alternatives

Polysight has real strengths—multi-platform coverage, quick metrics, freemium model. But it has gaps:

  • Freemium model feels incomplete. Free tier is limited, and paid tiers are pricey relative to what you get.

The best alternatives either go deeper (Polytragent, Alphascope) or go wider (multi-platform), but they all improve on at least one dimension. Let's compare them.
