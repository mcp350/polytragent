Why Consider Polysight Alternatives?

Polysight is a respectable AI market analysis tool at $49/month. It provides sentiment analysis, pattern matching, and basic trading signals. But it has clear limitations: no cross-market analysis, no Manifold integration, limited customization, and web-only (no mobile app). Most importantly, other tools deliver equivalent or better accuracy at lower cost.

Polytragent, in particular, offers 6-7% better accuracy through deeper research, and superior transparency. For traders looking to optimize beyond Polysight, there are five compelling alternatives that each win on specific dimensions.

Why it wins: Shift from Polysight's pure analysis to copy trading. If you've found one exceptional trader, PolyGun's $150/mo cost at 100 trades is similar to Polysight's $49/mo, but with verified trader track record instead of AI black box.

Pricing: 0.3% per copy trade + 2% first copy. Roughly $150/mo at 100 trades.

Best for: Traders valuing proven trader track records over AI predictions, those with identified elite traders to follow.

vs Polysight: Replaces analysis with trusted trader execution. Not better for accuracy, but different philosophy (trader vs algorithm).

#5

OkBet (Performance-Aligned Copy)

Why it wins: Performance-fee model means OkBet only profits when you do. Removes the fixed-cost burden of Polysight's $49/mo. If OkBet's curation is profitable, you break even or win. If not, zero cost.

Pricing: 5% of profits only. Free if unprofitable.

Best for: Traders wanting aligned incentives, those uncomfortable with monthly fees on potentially unprofitable tools.

vs Polysight: Different fee model (performance vs subscription), but doesn't offer AI analysis. More of a philosophical choice.
