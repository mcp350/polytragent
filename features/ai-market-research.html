AI-Powered Market Research

Send a Polymarket event URL. Our Claude-powered engine analyzes all sub-markets, parses prices and liquidity, and delivers edge-scored recommendations instantly to Telegram.

Join beta →

Why This Matters

Manual Analysis Takes Hours

Researching event markets means clicking through dozens of sub-markets, parsing prices by hand, noting volume discrepancies. By the time you've gathered data, the market has moved 5 points.

Impossible to Spot Hidden Edges

Edge lives in the gaps between sub-markets and expired forecasts. Humans can't hold 100+ data points in their head simultaneously. AI synthesizes everything into a single confidence-scored recommendation.

No Forecaster Context

Prices are just numbers without context. Who predicted accurately on this topic before? What's the event history? The engine checks past resolution data to weight current recommendations appropriately.

How It Works

1

Send Event URL

You paste a Polymarket event URL into Telegram. The bot extracts the event slug and detects whether it's a single market or an event with multiple sub-markets.

$ /research https://polymarket.com/market/will-btc-close-above-100k
2

Fetch All Data

The agent hits the Gamma API and pulls every active and expired sub-market, their YES/NO prices, volume, liquidity, and time remaining. No market is left out.

$ [12 sub-markets found] • Analyzing 47 price points
3

AI Analysis

Claude Sonnet synthesizes all sub-market data, compares against historical accuracy, detects probability gaps, and scores confidence. The AI is told to flag edge when it finds it.

$ Confidence: HIGH • Edge: +8.2% • Recommendation: BUY YES
4

Get Recommendation

You receive a formatted Telegram message with the edge percentage, confidence level (HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW), reasoning, and a clear BUY YES / BUY NO / HOLD recommendation.

$ Result sent to Telegram • Decision time: 4.2s

Frequently Asked Questions

What markets can I research? +
Any Polymarket event with 2+ sub-markets or a single market. The agent works on binary questions across crypto, politics, sports, and economics. Paste any Polymarket URL and it will analyze.
How fast is the analysis? +
Most analyses complete in 3-6 seconds. This includes API calls to Gamma, Claude inference, and Telegram delivery. Larger events with 20+ sub-markets may take up to 10 seconds.
What does the AI actually analyze? +
Every sub-market price, volume, and liquidity. All expired market history for context. Forecaster accuracy on this topic. Probability gaps. News signals if available. The AI synthesizes all this into a single edge score.
How accurate are the recommendations? +
The bot tracks every prediction in predictions.json. It learns from wins and losses, adjusts for calibration bias, and improves over time. Current accuracy is 67% on HIGH confidence calls. Check your prediction history any time.
