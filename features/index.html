Core Research Features

Polytragent combines five specialized analysis engines — market research, cross-platform comparison, prediction tracking, edge detection, and self-improving AI — to give you the competitive advantage on Polymarket.

Each feature is built to solve a specific part of the prediction market workflow: discovering edges, validating hypotheses, executing trades, and improving over time. Together they form a complete research and execution platform.

① PARSE
AI Market Research
Send a Polymarket event URL. Polytragent instantly analyzes every sub-market, scores them for edge, and delivers BUY YES / BUY NO / HOLD recommendations via Telegram.

② COMPARE
Cross-Platform Comparison
Compares Polymarket prices with Manifold Markets to find price divergences. When Poly says 40% and Manifold says 68%, that's your edge: +28%.
Learn more →
③ LEARN
Self-Improving Predictions
Tracks every prediction and its outcome. AI learns from mistakes, adjusts calibration biases, and knows which topics it's strongest at — and improves every day.

④ DETECT
Edge Detection Engine
Calculates the difference between market price and AI estimated true probability. Scores edge %, confidence level, and recommendation. No edge = HOLD.

⑤ TRACK
Prediction Tracking & Resolution
Every prediction logged with market context. Background resolver checks Polymarket API every 6 hours. Records outcomes, calculates P&L, and feeds data back for continuous learning.
