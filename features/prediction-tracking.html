Why This Matters

Predictions Without Tracking Are Just Guesses

If you don't track outcomes, you can't know if you're improving. Most forecasters make predictions and never check them. Polytragent logs everything so you always know exactly how your AI is performing.

No Feedback Loop Means No Learning

AI only improves when it gets feedback. Polytragent's background resolver checks market outcomes every 6 hours and updates the record. The AI sees wins and losses and learns from them in real-time.

P&L Without Context Is Meaningless

Just knowing you made money doesn't tell you why. Polytragent logs every prediction with context: market price, confidence level, reasoning. You see exactly which decisions worked and which didn't.

How It Works

1

Prediction Logged Instantly

When you get AI analysis, the bot stores to predictions.json: market ID, market title, AI forecast %, recommendation (BUY_YES/BUY_NO/HOLD), current Polymarket price, Manifold price if available, confidence level, and reasoning snippet.

$ Prediction logged • Market: BTC 100k • Forecast: 52% • Confidence: HIGH
2

Background Resolver Checks Outcomes

Every 6 hours, a scheduled task queries Polymarket for all unresolved predictions. When a market resolves (YES or NO), the resolver records the actual outcome and marks the prediction correct or wrong.

$ Resolver running... • Checked: 127 predictions • Resolved: 3 new outcomes
3

Calculate Hypothetical P&L

For each resolved prediction, the system calculates: if you had placed $100 at the recommendation price, what would your P&L be now? This tracks realized and unrealized gains/losses across your prediction history.

$ P&L calculated: +$8.40 | Accuracy on this call: 100% | Win rate: 67%
4

AI Learns and Improves

The AI reads the full prediction history and sees: which topics it got right/wrong, confidence levels that were accurate vs overconfident, recent win streaks vs losing runs. Next prediction run, it factors all this in and makes better calls.

Frequently Asked Questions

What gets logged per prediction? +
Market ID, title, AI forecast %, recommendation (BUY_YES/BUY_NO/HOLD), Polymarket price when logged, Manifold price if cross-listed, confidence level (HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW), reasoning snippet, timestamp, and any available context like news signals.
How often does resolution check run? +
The background resolver runs every 6 hours on a schedule. When it runs, it checks all unresolved predictions against the Polymarket API. New outcomes are recorded immediately. You can also trigger a manual check with /predict-track resolve-now.
Can I export my history? +
Yes. Use /predict-track export to download predictions.json as CSV or JSON. This includes all predictions, outcomes, P&L calculations, and metadata. Perfect for record-keeping, taxes, or external analysis.
How is P&L calculated? +
Hypothetical: assume you placed $100 at the market price when the prediction was logged. P&L = (outcome_value - entry_price) * 100. For BUY YES at 40% that resolves YES: gain = 100 * (1 - 0.40) = $60. For BUY NO at 40% that resolves YES: loss = 100 * 0.40 = -$40.
What if a market doesn't resolve? +
Markets that don't resolve stay in your prediction history as "UNRESOLVED". If a market is cancelled or reverted by the platform, it's marked as such. The AI doesn't learn from unresolved or cancelled predictions.
