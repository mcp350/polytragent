07 — FAQ

Common
questions.

Everything you need to know before running your first /research command.

What exactly does Polytragent do?
Polytragent is an AI research agent for Polymarket. You send it a Polymarket event URL via Telegram, and it instantly analyzes every sub-market, cross-references forecaster platforms like Manifold Markets, reviews its own past prediction accuracy, and delivers AI-powered edge recommendations — BUY YES, BUY NO, or HOLD — with confidence levels and reasoning. It's like having a team of analysts in your pocket.
How many data points does it actually analyze?
Millions. Every sub-market in the event (including expired ones for context), all matching Manifold Markets predictions, the full prediction history database with win/loss tracking, news velocity signals, and on-chain data. All of this is synthesized by Claude AI in under 10 seconds.
Does it learn from its mistakes?
Yes — this is the core advantage. Every prediction is logged with the market price, AI forecast percentage, confidence level, and reasoning. A background resolver runs every 6 hours checking for outcomes. The AI sees its full track record — win rate, calibration biases, strong/weak topics, recent performance — and adjusts future predictions accordingly.
Does Polytragent execute trades for me?
Polytragent is a research agent — it tells you what to buy, not buys for you. Each recommendation comes with a direct trade link to Polymarket so you can execute with one click. You stay in control of every trade.
What platforms does it cross-reference?
Currently Manifold Markets is the primary cross-reference source for forecaster comparison. The agent searches by event title, finds matching binary markets, and compares prices. When Polymarket and Manifold disagree significantly, that's where edge lives.
How do I use it?
Open Telegram, send /research followed by any Polymarket event URL. That's it. The agent handles everything — URL parsing, sub-market fetching, forecaster comparison, track record analysis, AI synthesis — and delivers the results right in your Telegram chat.
