07 — FAQ

Common
questions.

Everything you need to know before connecting your wallet and running your first trade.

Is my wallet safe with Polytragent?
Yes. Polytragent is fully non-custodial — we operate via the official Polymarket CLOB API and never hold your funds, store your private key, or control your wallet. Revoke access instantly from Polymarket settings at any time.
What's the minimum capital to start?
You can start with as little as $50 USDC on the Day Pass. For a diversified multi-market strategy we recommend $500+. Market making requires at least $200 per market for meaningful spread capture.
What returns can I realistically expect?
Market making targets 0.5–2% monthly with under 1% drawdown. Momentum strategies target 3–8% monthly with moderate volatility. These are benchmarks from 6 months of orderbook analysis — not guarantees. Results vary by market conditions, capital, and configuration.
Does Polytragent work across all Polymarket markets?
Yes — politics, sports, crypto, economics, and any active Polymarket market. The agent filters for minimum liquidity before entering any position, so it only trades markets where execution risk is low.
Can I pause the agent at any time?
Yes, immediately. Pausing halts all new order placement. Open positions are held until market resolution or manual close. No penalties, no lock-in on either plan.
Is automated trading on Polymarket allowed?
Yes. The Polymarket CLOB API is public and bot trading is explicitly permitted. Polytragent fully complies with Polymarket's terms of service. Users are responsible for compliance with their own local regulations.
