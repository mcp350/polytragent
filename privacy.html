Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

1. Introduction

Polytragent ("we," "our," or "us") operates the polytragent.com website and the Polytragent Telegram bot (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service. By accessing or using the Service, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Account & Identity Data: When you interact with our Telegram bot, we receive your Telegram user ID, username, and display name. If you subscribe to Degen Mode, we collect your email address and payment information through Stripe (our payment processor).

Wallet Data: If you create or import a wallet through the Service, your private keys are encrypted using AES-256 (Fernet) encryption and stored locally. We do not have access to your unencrypted private keys. Polytragent is a non-custodial service — you maintain sole control over your wallet and funds at all times.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the Service, including research queries, trading activity initiated through the bot, whale tracking preferences, and copy trade configurations.

Blockchain Data: We access publicly available blockchain data from the Polygon network and Polymarket APIs to provide whale tracking, market research, and copy trading features. This data is public by nature.

Device & Technical Data: When you visit our website, we may collect standard log information such as IP address, browser type, referring pages, and timestamps.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Service
  • Process subscriptions and payments through Stripe
  • Deliver whale tracking notifications and copy trade signals
  • Execute trades on your behalf when you enable auto copy trading (Degen Mode only)
  • Generate AI-powered market research and event analysis
  • Monitor usage to enforce tier limits (free vs. Degen Mode)
  • Improve and optimize the Service
  • Communicate with you about updates, features, or support

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may receive or process your data:

  • Stripe — Payment processing for Degen Mode subscriptions. Stripe's privacy policy governs their handling of your payment information.
  • Telegram — Bot platform and messaging. Subject to Telegram's privacy policy.
  • Polymarket / Polygon Network — Public blockchain data accessed for research, whale tracking, and trade execution.
  • OpenAI — AI model provider used for market research analysis. Research queries may be processed by OpenAI's API.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use essential cookies for functionality purposes, such as maintaining admin session state. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels. The Telegram bot does not use cookies.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Wallet private keys are encrypted using AES-256 Fernet encryption with PBKDF2 key derivation (480,000 iterations). However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account and usage data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. If you stop using the Service, we may retain certain data as required by law or for legitimate business purposes. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us via our support channel.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data. You may also have the right to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at t.me/polytragent.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in jurisdictions outside your country of residence. By using the Service, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries that may have different data protection laws than your jurisdiction.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, contact us at t.me/polytragent.
